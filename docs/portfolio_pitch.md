# Portfolio pitch — AgendaYield

## Elevator (30s)
AgendaYield is a yield cockpit for service agendas. It shows occupancy, no-show risk, confirmation backlog, lost revenue and recovery actions — so a studio owner can treat every empty slot as recoverable money, not just a calendar gap.

## Interview talking points
- Yield vs booking software: maximize paid occupancy, not only schedule creation
- Interpretable no-show risk (history + confirmation + lead time)
- Operational action list with estimated BRL impact
- Explicit non-goals: not an EMR, not a marketplace
- Path from CSV/Google Calendar import to automated reminders

## LinkedIn blurb
Built AgendaYield: an agenda yield and no-show recovery cockpit for clinics, salons and studios — occupancy, risk board, confirmation queue and recoverable revenue for portfolio-grade operational analytics.
